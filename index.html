Hwatu Tarot Guide

Hwatu card deck has 48 cards. There are 12 kinds with 4 cards each. Simply put, pair similar cards until the spread is empty. The 12 kinds and their meanings are shown below. If all 4 cards of a kind are in a pile, that kind is part of your fortune for the day.

  • Select 2 cards of the same kind to move them to the piles.
  • Unusable cards will be half visible.
  • Press the glowing face-down card to pull out a card.
  • Deck will be cycled until empty.
  • When all 48 cards are in the piles, you can check your result.
  • If the spread is impossible to empty, simply try again.
